Journey

Recollecting the steps that led up to today

  • 2000-2012

    Humble Beginnings

    Started schooling at The Mother's International School.

  • 2012-2016

    A Passion is Born

    While pursuing my Bachelors, I developed a keen sense of interest in Computer Science and specifically in Network Security and other computer security techniques in addition to software engineering and software development.

  • August 2016

    Choosing A Direction

    In 2016, I decided to take the plunge and to follow my interest in Computer Security full time by joining USC's Masters program to enhance my skill and understanding in the field of Network Security and Cryptography.

  • June 2017 - May 2018

    Breaking Into The Industry

    Started working as an Application Security Engineer intern at Green Dot Corporation as a part of my summer internship which I continued on as a co-op throughout my Fall and Spring semesters garnering much needed eperience in the Application Security sector while working on Industrly leading tools such as HPE Fortify. I also worked heavily on automating the vulnerability assesment and security scanning process, mostly in a C#/.NET/PowerShell environment.

  • May 2018

    Stepping Into The Real World

    I graduated from USC in May 2018 with my Master's Degree in Computer Science.

  • June 2018-Present

    Solving Real World Challenges

    In June 2018, I joined Visa Inc. as an Information Security Analyst at their Foster City Headquarters with the aim of solving real world problems in the area of Computer Security specifically focusing on Mobile Security.
